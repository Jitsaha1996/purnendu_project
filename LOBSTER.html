LOBSTER

Lobsters are a family (Nephropidae, synonym Homaridae[2]) of large marine crustaceans. Lobsters have long bodies with muscular tails, and live in crevices or burrows on the sea floor. Three of their five pairs of legs have claws, including the first pair, which are usually much larger than the others.
